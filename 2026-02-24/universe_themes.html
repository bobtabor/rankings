Methodology Notes:

  • Basket Calculation: Equal-weighted average of constituent stock returns
  • Basket Value: All baskets start at 1000 on the first day of the chart period, then compound the equal-weighted average daily returns of their constituents. This allows comparing baskets on an equal footing regardless of constituent stock prices - a basket ending at 1100 gained 10%, while one ending at 900 lost 10%.
  • Intraday Gain: Equal-weighted average of constituent intraday gains
  • Daily Gain: Equal-weighted average of constituent daily gains
